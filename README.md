## 📊 Overview
Analysis of coffee export volumes and trends for **Brazil**, **Colombia**, and **Peru** from 2020 to 2025 using official export data.

### Volume Analysis
| Country | Trend 2020-2025 | Highlight |
|---|---|---|
| **Brazil** | ↗️ Stable Growth | Peak at 3.1M bags in 2024 |
| **Colombia** | ➡️ Stable | Focus on premium Arabica |
| **Peru** | 🚀 Fastest Growth | Specialty coffee boom |

### Quality Indicators
- **🇨🇴 Colombia**: High cupping scores, 100% Arabica, specialty market focus
- **🇧🇷 Brazil**: Mix of Arabica & Robusta, competitive pricing for commercial blends  
- **🇵🇪 Peru**: Growing in organic & Fair Trade, targeting US/EU niche markets
